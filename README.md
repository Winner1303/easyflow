## About EasyFlow

EasyFlow is a cutting-edge web application designed to empower users across Africa by providing a seamless way to transfer and receive money, open and manage multiple accounts, and hold and manage all African currencies. Our platform integrates with national payment methods to ensure accessibility and convenience for everyone.

## Features

- **User-Friendly Interface:** Intuitive and easy-to-navigate design.
- **Multi-Currency Support:** Open and manage accounts in various African currencies.
- **Instant Money Transfers:** Quick and secure transfers between accounts and to other users.
- **Transaction History:** Detailed records of all your transactions.
- **Refill Accounts:** Easily refill accounts using national payment methods.
- **Real-Time Notifications:** Stay updated with instant notifications for all activities.
- **Robust Security:** Advanced security measures to protect your data and transactions.
- **Customer Support:** Dedicated support team to assist you with any queries.

## How It Works

1. **Sign Up:** Create an account with EasyFlow using our simple registration process.
2. **Open Accounts:** Open multiple accounts in different African currencies.
3. **Transfer Money:** Transfer money instantly between your accounts or to other EasyFlow users.
4. **Manage Finances:** Keep track of your transactions and account balances.
5. **Refill Accounts:** Use national payment methods to add funds to your accounts.
6. **Own Currencies:** Hold as many Afircan currencies as you want.
