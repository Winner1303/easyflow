Amazing Services & Features for you

We believe that everyone in Africa deserves access to secure, affordable, and convenient financial services. EasyFlow is committed to breaking down barriers and fostering financial inclusion across the continent. Manage Multiple Currencies with Ease.

Maintenance & Security

We've implement advanced maintainance and security features like multi-factor authentication, device binding, fraud detection, and real-time transaction alerts to provide users with peace of mind. Our security team is here to make sure your account(s) and funds stay safe and secure.

Swift Transfer

Worried about the time it takes to transfer and receive money ? Don't worry , we've got you covered. Transactions, are done from one account to another as fast as possible while following due protocoles, to make sure that your money is transfered to the right account. It's all for your security.

Own Multiples Accounts

You can open as many accounts as you need to manage your finances effectively. Refill your account(s) using national payment methods, making it easier than ever to add funds. Our user-friendly app allows you to conveniently manage all your accounts in one place anywhere, anytime.

24/7 Help & Support

Client satisfaction is at the core of everything we do. EasyFlow is dedicated to exceeding your expectations by providing exceptional customer service. Our friendly and knowledgeable team is readily available to address your inquiries and ensure a smooth experience.

About What We Do & Who We Are

EasyFlow is revolutionizing the way Africans manage their money. We are an online pan-African money transfer designed to make financial transactions easier, faster, and more secure for everyone. We are committed to financial inclusion, empowering people across the continent to take control of their finances.
